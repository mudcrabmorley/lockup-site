Lock Up Industries has years of experience in all aspects of pressed metal fire rated door frames and non-rated frames to suit existing or new applications. Lock Up Industries has the experience and qualifications to complete the project professionally and efficiently.

Our fully equipped workshop has a guillotine and press folders for all sheet metal applications, whether it be for mild steel, stainless steel, or custom frame sections to suit clients' requests.

Pressed metal frames can be supplied in knock-down sectional form or fully welded and assembled.

On-site welding repairs and partial frame section replacements can save considerable cost and disruption to existing finishes. Rusted lower frame sections can be replaced with stainless steel matched profiles.

Our project experience includes shopping centres, government institutions, high-rise buildings, and commercial/industrial complexes.
